# One-Day Meal Plan for Marathon Training

Here is a one-day meal plan for an athlete aiming to maximize carbohydrate loading and hydration for marathon training endurance performance:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3,200 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (130g), Fat (85g), Carbs (460g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (6:30 AM):** Oatmeal (100g dry weight) with banana (150g), honey (25g), and whole milk (250g). Side of whole wheat toast (60g) with almond butter (20g).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 95g

* **Pre-Workout Snack (8:30 AM):** Energy bar (65g) and sports drink (500ml).
    * Protein: 8g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 70g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM):** Brown rice (150g cooked) with grilled chicken breast (140g), steamed carrots (100g), peas (80g), and olive oil drizzle (15g). Orange juice (250ml).
    * Protein: 42g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 95g

* **Afternoon Snack (3:30 PM):** Bagel (85g) with jam (30g), Greek yogurt (170g), and berries (100g).
    * Protein: 20g, Fat: 4g, Carbs: 75g

* **Dinner (7:00 PM):** Whole wheat pasta (120g cooked) with lean ground turkey (130g) in tomato sauce (150g), mixed vegetables (100g), and parmesan cheese (15g).
    * Protein: 32g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 85g

* **Evening Snack (9:00 PM):** Bagel with honey (40g) and chamomile tea (200ml).
    * Protein: 10g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 60g

* **Hydration Throughout Day:** 3-4 liters of water, electrolyte drink (1 liter), and herbal tea.

This meal plan prioritizes complex carbohydrates for sustained energy, adequate protein for muscle recovery, and consistent hydration to support marathon training demands.
